# Tarstate research notes

This folder holds non-normative investigation that may inform a future Tarstate
contract. Research notes describe evidence, alternatives, unresolved behavior,
and acceptance questions. They are not promises that a feature will be built.

Once a design is accepted, its observable behavior belongs in `docs/specs`, and
the research note should either become a short source record or be deleted.
